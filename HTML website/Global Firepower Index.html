Global Firepower Index

What is the Global Firepower Index?

Since 2006, the global firepower index is a form of analitical data that measures a countries war making capability.

What does that mean for the UK?

As of this Current year, 2021, the United Kingdom ranks 8th as the most powerful countries in the world. The power index uses a number based measurement that is used to rank the country. 0.0000 is considered perfect.
